Miles's Smack N' Cheese

Ingredients

  • 8 oz elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 1/2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp mustard powder
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Cook 8 oz elbow macaroni according to package directions, then drain.
  2. In a saucepan, melt 1/4 cup butter over medium heat. Stir in 2 1/2 tbsp all-purpose flour, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp ground black pepper, 1/4 tsp paprika, 1/4 tsp garlic powder, 1/4 tsp onion powder, and 1/4 tsp mustard powder.
  3. Gradually add 3 cups milk, whisking constantly. Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly. Reduce heat and cook for 2 more minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Remove saucepan from heat and stir in 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese and 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  5. Combine the cooked macaroni and cheese sauce in a baking dish.
  6. Mix 1/2 cup breadcrumbs and 2 tbsp melted butter, then sprinkle over the macaroni and cheese.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown on top.
