Miles's Buttermilk Fried Chicken Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast halves
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 1 tbsp salt (for marinade)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper (for breading)
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt (for breading)
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Milk & 1 egg for egg wash
  • Oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper to taste for aioli
  • Brioche buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, cheese, bacon

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in 1 cup buttermilk, 1/4 cup hot sauce, 1 tbsp salt, and 1 tsp black pepper for 30 min.
  2. Mix dry ingredients: 1 cup flour, 1 tsp garlic & onion powder, 3 tbsp powdered sugar, 1/2 tsp paprika, 1 tsp black pepper, 1/2 tsp chili powder, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/2 tsp baking powder.
  3. Prepare egg wash with enough milk to submerge the chicken and 1 egg.
  4. Coat chicken in breading and egg wash.
  5. Fry in oil at 350°F (180°C) for 6-8 min, then drain.
  6. Mix 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 2 minced garlic cloves, 1 tbsp lemon juice, and salt & pepper to taste for aioli.
  7. Assemble sandwich with toasted buns, aioli, lettuce, tomato, chicken, cheese, and bacon.
